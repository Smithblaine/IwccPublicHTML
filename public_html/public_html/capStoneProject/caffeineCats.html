A Little About Us

Caffeine cats is a new coffee shop experience opening in the Dog Patch of San Fransisco. We will feature exquisite exclusive beans from Guatemala. We have personal and direct connections with the farms growing our coffee and can assure our guests that their coffee is fair trade and grown with organic practices. All of our coffee beans are roasted in house using vintage turn of the century equipment. Our start up is still in the planning stages and we are looking for an advertisement group to help us develop our brand visually.

We make all of our coffee with love and its all made fresh

Our Store

We have a small store that sells coffee and other soft drink beverages. Along with donuts and other snacks. Our store is perfect for get together drinks with friends to sit down and have some good catching up conversations with friends.
